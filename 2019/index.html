Registration for the Brainhack DC 2019 is now open!

The Brainhack DC 2019 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2019 that is organized by Cameron Craddock . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: November 16-17, 2019

Costs: FREE!

Location: Georgetown University, Washington DC, USA

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2019 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack MatterMost project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas. Search for our channel: "bhg-washingtondc"
